Conor Sloan - Software Engineer

Experienced London-based multi-discipline Software Engineer @ Apple. Previously Amazon, BT, Oracle.

I tend towards the following habits; they provide an outline of how I like to work.

  • I ask lots of questions. Sometimes, too many! Though I often prefer consulting good documentation.
  • I love to design systems and architectures, though I get itchy fingers if I haven't coded in a while.
  • So far, I have chosen adaptability over specialisation. I learn fast, but I'm not a world-leading expert in one area.
  • I believe strongly in developing the 'softer skills'. I like to present, discuss ideas, and build useful relationships.
  • I value personal connections. It matters to me that my colleagues are happy, and I will work in aid of that goal.
  • I engage actively in performance review processes, since I believe them to be extemely valuable if done right.
  • The trait I value most highly is pragmatism. I am rarely stuck in my ways, and I hope to see that in others.
For a broader picture of my personality, the description of the ENFP personality type doesn't do a bad job!
